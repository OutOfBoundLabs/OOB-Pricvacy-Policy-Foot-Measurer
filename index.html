Privacy Policy

FootMeasurer  ·  Last updated: May 27, 2026

Overview

FootMeasurer does not collect or transmit any user data to external servers. All data stays exclusively on the user's device.

Data stored locally

The app saves only the following on your device:

  • profile name provided by the user (nickname or first name)
  • shoe size determined by the app

This data is used solely to operate the app and personalize settings. It is never shared with third parties.

Foot photo processing

To determine shoe size, the app asks you to take a photo of your foot.

The photo is processed entirely on your device — it is never sent to any server or shared with third parties. Once the measurement is complete, the photo is permanently deleted from the app's memory.

Camera access is required solely for this purpose.

Deleting your data

You can delete all stored data at any time by clearing the app's data in your device settings, or by uninstalling the app, which permanently removes all saved data.

External links

The app may open external web links in your device's default browser.
